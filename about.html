About Noah Hospital

Leading the way in healthcare excellence since 1985

Our Mission

To provide exceptional healthcare services with compassion and integrity, while advancing medical innovation and fostering a healthier community. We are committed to:

  • Delivering patient-centered care with the highest standards of quality and safety
  • Advancing medical knowledge through research and education
  • Promoting wellness and preventive care in our communities
  • Ensuring accessibility to healthcare services for all

Our Journey

1985

Noah Hospital founded with a 50-bed facility in Riyadh

1995

Expansion to 200 beds and opening of specialized cardiac center

2005

Launch of international patient program and new research center

2015

Opening of multiple centers across Saudi Arabia

2024

Celebrating excellence with state-of-the-art facilities across 7 locations
